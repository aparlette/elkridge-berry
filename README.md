# Elkridge Hurricanes 12U Baseball Website

A simple, focused website for the Elkridge Hurricanes 2025-26 12U Berry rising travel baseball team.

## Features

- **Home Page**: Introduction to the team
- **Tryouts Page**: Detailed tryout information including dates, location, and requirements
- **Registration**: Direct link to Google Forms for tryout registration
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works great on phones and tablets
- **University of Miami Colors**: Orange (#F47321) and Green (#005030)

## Tryout Information

- **Dates**: Thursday July 10 & 17, 2025 at 6:00 PM
- **Location**: Elkridge Landing Middle School, Field 16
- **Age**: Players born after May 1, 2013
- **Highlight**: Cooperstown Dreams Park Summer 2026

## Files

- `index.html` - Complete single-page website with all content
